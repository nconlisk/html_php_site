Welcome to the homepage of the Cardiovascular Physics group. Under the direction of Prof. Peter Hoskins, and based within the College of Medicine and Veterinary Medicine at the University of Edinburgh, the group carries out cutting edge computational and experimental research in the areas of medical physics and cardiovascular biomechanics.



Particular research interests of the group are (click on the links to learn more):

        • Modelling of Cerebral Aneurysms
        • Magnetic Resonance Elastography
        • Doppler Ultrasound
